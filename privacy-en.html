Privacy Policy - Rutino Mio

Last updated: 2026-02-04

1. Data Controller

The data controller is Gregorio Visocchi Labs.
Contact: GregorioVisocchiLabs@gmail.com

2. Scope and Nature of the App

Rutino Mio is an app for tracking daily routines and habits. The app does not require an account and does not register users.

3. What Data We Process

3.1 Data Stored Locally (on the device)

  • routine names, schedules, and categories,
  • routine completion status,
  • user preferences (language, theme, notification settings, ad consent).

This data is stored locally in SQLite and SharedPreferences and is not synced to the cloud.

3.2 Local Notifications

The app schedules local notifications to remind you about routines. Notifications are handled entirely on the device and do not require any external service.

3.3 Data Export and Import

The app allows exporting and importing routine data as a JSON file. The file is stored locally on the device. We do not have access to it.

3.4 Voice Input â€“ Microphone

The app allows using voice dictation to add routine names. When you activate voice input, the app requests access to the microphone. Audio is processed by the speech recognition system of your device's operating system (Google on Android, Apple on iOS) and may be sent to their servers for processing. We do not have access to recorded audio or transcription results.

3.5 Ads - Google AdMob

The app uses the Google AdMob SDK. In connection with displaying ads, Google may process, among others:

  • the device advertising identifier,
  • IP address,
  • device and operating system information,
  • ad interaction data.

Details: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Purposes and Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Service provision (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): local data necessary for the app's features to function.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): for personalized ads, if enabled according to user settings and consent.
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): displaying non-personalized ads and maintaining app funding.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with other parties. The exception is data processed by Google as part of Google AdMob.

6. Transfer Outside the EEA

Google may process data outside the European Economic Area. Transfers are carried out in accordance with mechanisms required by law; details are available in Google's privacy policy.

7. Retention Period

  • Local data is retained until deleted by the user or until the app is uninstalled.
  • Ad data is retained in accordance with Google's policies.

8. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • access your data,
  • rectify your data,
  • delete your data,
  • restrict processing,
  • data portability (with respect to local data),
  • object to processing,
  • withdraw consent (without affecting the lawfulness of processing prior to withdrawal).

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority.

9. Security

User data is stored locally on the device. The app does not transmit user data to our servers. Data may only be transmitted to Google services for ads (AdMob), if the user makes use of them.

10. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent/guardian and believe that a child has provided data, please contact us.

11. Policy Changes

Changes to this policy will be published in this document by updating the date.
